🚇 Delhi NCR Metro – Route Map, Bus App
📋 Overview
Delhi NCR Metro ("App") is an independent, informational travel guide app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy.
📊 Information We Collect
We do NOT collect:
• Your name, email or personal details
• Account registration or login
• Payment information
• Call logs, contacts or messages

We may collect (via third-party SDKs):
• Device type and OS version (for ads only)
• Approximate location (only when you tap "Find Nearest Station" — GPS feature)
• Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics
📍 Location Data
Location is accessed ONLY when you use the "Nearest Stations (GPS)" feature. We do not store, share or track your location at any time. Location permission is optional — the app works fully without it.
📢 Advertising
This app may display ads via Google AdMob. Google may use your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can opt out via your device settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
🔗 Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
• Google AdMob — for displaying ads
• Google Analytics / Firebase — for anonymous usage stats
• Google Fonts — for typography

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.
👶 Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
🔐 Data Security
We do not store any personal data on our servers. All metro data (stations, fares, timings) is stored locally within the app and does not require an internet connection.
⚠️ Important Disclaimer
This app is an independent, privately developed informational guide. It is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to DMRC (Delhi Metro Rail Corporation), the Government of India, Delhi Government, or any government authority.

All data is sourced from publicly available official sources (delhimetrorail.com). Always verify fares, timings and routes from official DMRC sources before travel.
🔄 Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at your registered developer email on Google Play Console.
